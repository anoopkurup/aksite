## What works
- **Key pages (home, about, clear, pipeline-reality-check) are strong E-E-A-T**: first-person-singular voice, plainly-stated prices (₹25K, ₹2.5L), honest "not for you if" / "I don't promise a signed client" caveats, named outcomes ("70% → 94% admissions conversion").
